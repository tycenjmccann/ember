How it works

Hand it off at your desk. Pick it up at the campfire.

One handoff, three devices, zero babysitting. The build keeps burning while you're somewhere better than your chair.

1 Give it a repo + task
⌨️

Brief it from your laptop

Point Ember at a repo and describe the work. It clones, plans, codes, builds, and opens a PR — server-side on a per-session micro-VM. Or port a live local Claude Code / Codex session straight up.

laptop → cloud
2 It runs warm in your AWS
🔥

Close the lid

The session keeps running on an Amazon Bedrock AgentCore micro-VM with a persistent EFS workspace — banked and warm in your own account. Go outside. The loop doesn't need you.

your VPC · warm
3 Resume from any device
📱

Open a deep link on the web or your phone — same workspace, same context, still running. Course-correct over lunch, tap "ship it," then pull the grown session back home at night.

Warm sessions

A banked fire doesn't go out when you walk away.

WarmLive micro-VM, holding context and heat. Resume instantly. ~0.1¢/idle-min
IdleCoals dimming — workspace persisted to EFS, ready to flare back. parked
ColdGone out, nothing lost. Reopen and it rebuilds from the checkpoint. $0

Every session maps to a real warm/idle/cold state — it isn't a metaphor we bolted on, it's the mechanic. Close the lid and the embers hold their heat; reopen on any device and it flares straight back.

  • →Resumable everywhere. Persistent EFS workspace + deep links. Laptop, web, phone — same session.
  • →Bring your own plan. Connect the Claude Pro/Max or ChatGPT plan you already pay for — $0 marginal LLM cost.
  • →Or Bedrock, in-VPC. Pay-per-token, fully in your account, for compliance-restricted repos.
  • →Laptop ⇄ cloud handoff. port a live session up, pull the grown one back home.
