A mini brand-kit sheet for ONE logo — single image, white background, 2x2 grid of 4 numbered
cells (1–4). All four cells show the SAME logo mark (from the reference image) in 4 different
formats. Keep the mark itself identical to the reference: a charred TEXTURED-BARK log at a 3/4
angle, its glowing round cut END a live ember with a ">_" terminal prompt, resting on a bed of
CHUNKY NATURAL (irregular, faceted, not round) glowing coals.

4. HORIZONTAL LOCKUP — the no-circle mark on the LEFT at small size, with the lowercase

   tracking). The full primary logo lockup. On white.

STYLE: flat, simple, professional vector logo, consistent line-weight. Warm orange accent only
where the reference has it (glow + coals + terminal); cells 1/2/4 keep the orange, cell 3 is
pure black-on-white. NO photorealism, NO 3D, NO drop shadows beyond the badge glow, clean and
production-ready. Small cell number only (plus the actual "ember" wordmark in cell 4). Generous
white space. This is a finalist brand kit — make every format crisp and shippable.
