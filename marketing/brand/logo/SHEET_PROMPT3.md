A clean logo design exploration sheet — single image, white background, tidy 4x3 grid of 12
numbered cells (1–12). Each cell = ONE distinct simple minimal vector logo mark for a brand
called "ember".

ANCHOR (every cell, see reference image): a bold simple flat black SIDE-VIEW CHARRED LOG, and
its glowing cut END is a live ember forming a TERMINAL PROMPT — a ">" or ">_" / block cursor
glowing in the charred ring (the log end = a warm coal = a coding terminal). Keep that fusion
in all 12.

THIS ROUND — add campfire CONTEXT/SURROUNDINGS around that log (this is the variation axis):
explore the log + terminal-ember end SITTING IN a campfire setting. Try, across the 12:
- the log resting on / nestled in a BED OF HOT COALS (small simple coal lumps glowing beneath)
- inside a FIRE RING of simple stones (circle of pebbles around it)
- crossed with a second log (two logs, the front end glowing the > )
- a low banked-fire mound the log sits on
- a few minimal rising sparks / a single wisp of smoke above
- a circular badge/emblem lockup: the log-with-> centered in a round campfire-ring badge
- a couple kept MINIMAL (just the log + > , little or no surroundings) for contrast
- one or two combining the log-end ">" with a lowercase "e"

STYLE (critical): flat, simple, monoline / consistent line-weight — a designer's b&w concept
sheet. Mostly solid black on pure white; the glowing ember end + coals may use ONE warm orange
accent, kept minimal. NO heavy gradients, NO 3D, NO photorealism, NO drop shadows. Bold,
geometric, iconic, reproducible at any size, reads instantly at small sizes.

BRAND: "ember" — an open-source coding agent that keeps running in your own cloud while you
step away; "keep your session warm." A banked charred log holds heat quietly (NOT a flame, NOT
a roaring campfire). Warm, outdoorsy, calm, confident, developer-native. 12 distinct takes.
Small cell number only, no other text, lots of white space. Make them shippable simplified marks.
