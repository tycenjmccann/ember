A clean logo design exploration sheet — single image, white background, tidy 4x3 grid of 12
numbered cells (1–12). Each cell = ONE distinct simple minimal vector logo mark for "ember".

LOCKED CONCEPT (every one of the 12 is a variation of THIS — see the two reference images):
A single CHARRED LOG seen at a 3/4 angle, foreshortened, the round cut END facing the viewer
lower-left (exactly the shape/angle/proportion of the reference logs). The glowing cut END is a
live ember forming a TERMINAL PROMPT — a ">" chevron or ">_" prompt / block cursor glowing
inside the round coal face. Keep this exact log shape and glowing-terminal-end in all 12.

TWO CHANGES from the reference this round:
1. TEXTURED BARK — the log surface must look like NATURAL ROUGH BARK, not a smooth black
   cylinder. Add simple bark texture: short bark ridges / cracks / striations / knots / a
   woodgrain line or two along the log, a slightly irregular bumpy silhouette. Still flat and
   minimal and iconic — suggest texture with a few confident marks, do NOT make it noisy or
   photorealistic. Rugged, hand-hewn firewood feel.
2. BED OF EMBERS/COALS beneath it — the log RESTS ON a small bed of glowing hot coals: simple
   rounded coal lumps clustered under the log, a few glowing warm orange, a soft warm glow
   pooling beneath. Like the log was just set on the fire's coals.

VARY ACROSS THE 12: amount/style of bark texture (ridges vs cracks vs knots vs grain lines);
the terminal glyph (> vs >_ vs block cursor); how many coals / how much glow beneath; a tiny
rising spark or smoke wisp on some; tighter vs looser framing; one or two as a round
campfire-badge enclosing the whole scene. Always the SAME 3/4 foreshortened log + glowing
terminal end as the constant.

STYLE (critical): flat, simple, monoline-friendly, consistent line-weight — a professional
designer's b&w concept sheet. Mostly SOLID BLACK log on pure WHITE; the glowing terminal end
and the coals use ONE warm ORANGE accent, kept minimal. NO heavy gradients, NO 3D render, NO
photorealism, NO drop shadows. Bold, geometric, iconic, reproducible at any size, reads at
small sizes. Small cell number only, no other text, generous white space.

BRAND: "ember" — open-source coding agent that keeps running in your own cloud while you step
away; "keep your session warm." A banked charred log holds heat quietly (NOT a flame, NOT a
roaring fire). Warm, outdoorsy, rugged, developer-native. 12 distinct shippable simplified marks.
