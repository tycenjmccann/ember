**3 / WARM SESSIONS**

Every session is a persistent micro-VM + workspace (Amazon Bedrock AgentCore + EFS).

warm · idle · cold — actual states, with little glowing dots in the sidebar.

Start at your desk. Course-correct from your phone on the train. Pull it home at night.

Same session id. Full history. Nothing lost.
